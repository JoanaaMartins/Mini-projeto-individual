Drinks

DRINKS (NON-ALCOHOLIC)

Main course

Lemonade: A classic and versatile option that complements a wide range of main courses.
Iced Tea: Whether sweetened or unsweetened, iced tea is a refreshing choice that pairs well with many dishes.
Sparkling Water with Citrus: The effervescence and citrus flavor can cleanse the palate between bites.

Meat

Cranberry Juice: The tartness of cranberry juice can balance the richness of meat dishes.
Ginger Ale: The subtle spiciness of ginger ale can enhance the flavors of various meats.
Tomato Juice: Especially good with red meats, tomato juice adds acidity and depth.

Fish

Cucumber Mint Cooler: Cucumber and mint provide a fresh and cool contrast to the flavors of fish.
Coconut Water: A light and tropical option that pairs well with the delicate taste of many fish dishes.
Iced Green Tea: The mild, grassy notes of green tea complement fish without overpowering.

Vegetarian

Hibiscus Iced Tea: Hibiscus tea's floral and slightly tart profile pairs well with a variety of vegetarian dishes.
Sparkling Water with Berries: The natural sweetness of berries can enhance the flavors of vegetarian meals.
Lemon Basil Soda: The citrusy and herbal combination works well with vegetable-based dishes.

Soups

Vegetable Broth: Light and savory, vegetable broth complements a wide range of soups.
Apple Cider: Especially good with autumn soups, the sweetness of apple cider adds a comforting element.
Mint Infused Water: A simple and refreshing choice to cleanse the palate between spoonfuls.

Salads

Citrus Infused Water: The brightness of citrus can elevate the flavors of salads.
Cucumber Lemonade: A crisp and cool option that pairs well with the freshness of salads.
Iced Peppermint Tea: Peppermint tea adds a refreshing and slightly minty note to accompany salads.

Dessert

Hot Chocolate: Especially good with chocolate-based desserts, hot chocolate adds warmth and richness.
Chai Latte: The spices in chai complement a variety of desserts, from cakes to pastries.
Vanilla Milkshake: A classic choice that pairs well with a wide range of sweet treats.
