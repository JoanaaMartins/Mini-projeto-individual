Starter Dish

Duck Confit Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Duck confit, shredded
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Carrots, julienned
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Mix shredded duck confit with hoisin sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, green onions, and cilantro.
  2. Soften spring roll wrappers and fill with the duck mixture, shredded cabbage, and julienned carrots.
  3. Roll tightly and seal with water.
  4. Fry until golden brown and serve with a dipping sauce made

Meat

Beef Wellington:

Ingredients:

  • Beef tenderloin
  • Puff pastry
  • Prosciutto or Parma ham
  • Mushroom duxelles (mushrooms, shallots, garlic, thyme)
  • Dijon mustard
  • Egg wash (beaten egg with a splash of water)

Instructions:

  1. Sear beef tenderloin on all sides until browned.
  2. Brush with Dijon mustard and wrap with prosciutto.
  3. Spread mushroom duxelles over puff pastry.
  4. Place beef on the pastry and wrap, sealing the edges.
  5. Brush with egg wash and bake until golden.
  6. Rest before slicing into elegant portions.

Fish

Pan-Seared Sea Bass with Lemon Caper Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Sea bass fillets
  • Flour for dredging
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic, minced
  • Capers, drained
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • White wine
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Dredge sea bass fillets in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a pan.
  3. Pan-sear the sea bass until golden on both sides.
  4. Remove fish from the pan. In the same pan, add garlic, capers, lemon juice, and white wine.
  5. Simmer until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Pour the lemon caper sauce over the sea bass.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Vegetarian

Eggplant Parmesan Towers:

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant, sliced
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and grill or bake until tender.
  2. In a baking dish, layer marinara sauce, grilled eggplant, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  3. Repeat the layers until you have a tower.
  4. Bake until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Soups

Lobster Bisque:

Ingredients:

  • Lobster tails
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Carrots, chopped
  • Celery, chopped
  • Tomato paste
  • Brandy
  • Fish stock
  • Heavy cream
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Saut√© onions, carrots, and celery in a pot until softened.
  2. Add tomato paste and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in brandy and let it simmer to cook off the alcohol.
  4. Add lobster tails and fish stock, then simmer until the lobster is cooked.
  5. Remove lobster tails, extract the meat, and set aside.
  6. Blend the soup until smooth and return to the pot.
  7. Add heavy cream, cayenne pepper, and the extracted lobster meat.
  8. Simmer until heated through. Garnish with fresh parsley.
  9. Simmer until the flavors meld together.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Salads

Grilled Peach and Prosciutto Salad

Ingredients:

  • Ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  • Prosciutto slices
  • Arugula
  • Goat cheese
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Grill peach halves until grill marks appear.
  2. Arrange arugula on a serving platter.
  3. Top with grilled peaches, prosciutto slices, and crumbled goat cheese.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Dessert

Opera Cake:

Ingredients:

  • Almond Joconde (almond sponge)
  • Coffee Buttercream
  • Chocolate Ganache
  • Coffee Syrup
  • Dark chocolate for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Bake thin layers of almond joconde. Cut into rectangles to fit the size of your desired cake.
  2. Make a coffee syrup by dissolving sugar in strong coffee.
  3. Layer almond sponge with coffee syrup, coffee buttercream, and repeat.
  4. Finish with a layer of chocolate ganache on top.
  5. Chill the cake to set the layers.
  6. Once set, decorate the top with melted dark chocolate in a decorative pattern.
  7. Chill until ready to serve. Slice carefully for a stunning layered dessert.
