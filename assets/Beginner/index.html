Beginner

Starter dish

Mozzarella Sticks

Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella cheese sticks
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Marinara sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Cut mozzarella sticks in half.
  2. Coat each piece in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and coat with bread crumbs.
  3. Freeze for 30 minutes, then bake until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping.

Meat

Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Bread crumbs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions:

  1. Coat chicken breasts in bread crumbs and bake until golden.
  2. Top with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  4. Serve over spaghetti or your favorite pasta.

Fish

Crispy Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillets
  • Dijon mustard
  • Bread crumbs
  • Lemon zest
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven and place salmon fillets on a baking sheet.
  2. Mix Dijon mustard with olive oil and brush over the salmon.
  3. Combine bread crumbs with lemon zest and sprinkle over the salmon.
  4. Bake until the salmon is cooked through and the topping is crispy.

Vegetarian

Spinach and Feta Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • Portobello mushrooms
  • Spinach, chopped
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and brush with olive oil.
  2. Sauté minced garlic in olive oil, then add chopped spinach until wilted
  3. Stuff each mushroom with the spinach mixture and top with feta.
  4. Bake until mushrooms are tender.

Soup

Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Vegetable broth
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in a pot until fragrant.
  2. Add canned tomatoes and vegetable broth.
  3. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Stir in fresh basil, and season with salt and pepper.

Salads

Classic Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Caesar dressing

Instructions:

  1. Toss chopped lettuce with croutons in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss until well coated.
  3. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  4. Serve chilled.

Desserts

No-Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 cups crushed graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together peanut butter, melted butter, powdered sugar, and graham cracker crumbs until well combined.
  2. Press the mixture into the bottom of a lined 9x9 inch baking pan.
  3. Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave or on the stove, and spread evenly over the peanut butter mixture.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set. Once set, cut into bars and serve.
