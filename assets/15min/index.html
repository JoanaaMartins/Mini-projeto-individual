Meat

Quick Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 8 small taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese (for toppings)

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat.
  2. Drain excess fat and add taco seasoning following the packet instructions.
  3. Stir in salsa and cook until heated through.
  4. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  5. Spoon the beef mixture into taco shells and top with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.

Fish

Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp, cook until they turn pink..
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over the shrimp, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

Vegetarian

Spinach and Feta Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, sauté spinach in olive oil until wilted.
  2. Place a tortilla in the pan, sprinkle with mozzarella, feta, and sautéed spinach.
  3. Top with another tortilla and cook until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is Golden brown on both sides.

Soups

Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, combine crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Simmer over medium heat.
  2. Stir in heavy cream and chopped basil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.

Salads

Greek Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, olives, feta, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.

Dessert

Greek Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. .In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1-2 minutes, or until the cake has risen and set in the middle.
  4. Let it cool for a minute before enjoying.
